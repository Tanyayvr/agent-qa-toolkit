What this article requires

A strong template does not try to replace full legal review. It provides the technical backbone: what the system is, where it runs, what evidence exists, and what still requires human completion.

FAQ

Can Annex IV be filled from evidence alone?

No. It needs human-authored sections too.

What is the value of automation here?

Automation reduces the gap between technical runs and documentation references.

Should counsel still review the final package?

Yes. Technical structure and legal sufficiency are different tasks.
