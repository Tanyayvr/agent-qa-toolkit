FAQ

Do I need to share my AI model or data?

No. The core evidence workflow is self-hosted. Your runs stay in your environment.

What is the difference between Free OSS and the Launch Pack?

Free OSS is self-serve: you use the builder, GitHub repo, docs, and demo proof on your own. The Launch Pack adds setup help and first-package delivery for one AI system.

What is a supported agent in the monthly plans?

A supported agent is an agent that sits inside the paid support scope. Monthly plans cover ongoing help, updates, and recurring guidance for that number of agents.

Do monthly plans include done-for-you setup for every new agent?

No. Done-for-you initial setup is sold via the Launch Pack or Enterprise implementation. Monthly plans cover support after onboarding.

Is there an early-bird offer?

Yes. The Launch Pack has a 40% early-bird discount through August 1, 2026.

Does this replace legal counsel?

No. The site and toolkit generate technical evidence and documentation structure. Legal teams still own legal interpretation.
