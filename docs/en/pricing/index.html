FAQ

Do I need to share my AI model or data?

No. The core evidence workflow is self-hosted. Your runs stay in your environment.

What should I start with?

Start free with Builder and the EU starter if you are still evaluating fit or can run the workflow yourself. Use paid help when one system needs a first real package. Use enterprise support when the work is broader or more formal.

What exactly does paid help buy?

Paid help helps your team reach the first real package on its own system faster. It does not replace legal review, governance review, or final sign-off.

When should I move beyond self-serve?

Move beyond self-serve when one real system needs a real package and your team does not want to piece the path together alone.

Does this replace legal counsel?

No. The site and toolkit generate technical evidence and documentation structure. Legal teams still own legal interpretation.
