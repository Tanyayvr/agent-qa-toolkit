EU AI Act Article 9 - Risk Management System Template

Article 9 requires a documented risk management system that is planned, maintained, and updated throughout the lifecycle of a high-risk AI system. Teams usually understand the policy intent. The hard part is proving that the process is actually tested and not just described.

Section guide

SectionMeaningEvidence
Risk inventoryList concrete harms, failure modes, and affected users.Per-case risk level, gate recommendation, scanner results.
Mitigation controlsExplain which controls reduce the highest risks.Security findings, human-approval path, blocked actions.
Validation cadenceShow how often the risk process is tested.Recurring evidence packs and monitoring exports.
Residual risk reviewCapture what remains unresolved and who signs off.Release review and oversight summary.
